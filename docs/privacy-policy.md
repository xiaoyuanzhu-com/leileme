# Privacy Policy — LeiLeMe (累了么)

*Last updated: April 10, 2026*

## Overview

LeiLeMe ("the App") is a daily recovery assessment tool for iOS. This privacy policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

**The short version:** All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

---

## Data We Access

### Health Data (via Apple HealthKit)

With your permission, the App reads the following data from Apple Health:

- **Resting Heart Rate** — used to assess cardiovascular recovery
- **Heart Rate Variability (HRV)** — used to assess autonomic nervous system recovery
- **Sleep Analysis** — used to assess sleep quality and duration

This data is:
- **Read-only** — the App does not write to Apple Health
- **Processed locally** — never transmitted off your device
- **Optional** — the App functions without HealthKit authorization (6 of 9 measures do not require it)

### Assessment Data

The App stores your daily assessment results (reaction time, tap speed, grip strength, balance, perceived energy, mood, and recovery scores) in a local database on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

This data is:
- **Stored locally only** — on your device's storage
- **Never transmitted** — no network requests are made by the App
- **Exportable** — you can export all your data as CSV or JSON at any time via Settings
- **Deletable** — uninstalling the App removes all stored data

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- No personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
- No device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
- No location data
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No crash reporting to external services
- No advertising identifiers
- No cookies or web tracking

---

## Third-Party Sharing

**None.** We do not share any data with third parties. There are no third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, or advertising networks in the App.

---

## Tracking

The App does **not** track you across other companies' apps or websites. The App does not use the App Tracking Transparency framework because there is nothing to track.

---

## Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
- Data is protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
- No cloud sync, no remote backups by the App
- iCloud device backups may include App data per your device settings

---

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The App does not collect any personal data from any user.

---

## Your Rights

- **Access:** Export all your data anytime via Settings > Export Data
- **Deletion:** Uninstall the App to delete all data, or use Recalibrate Baseline to reset assessment history
- **Portability:** Export supports CSV and JSON formats
- **Consent withdrawal:** Revoke HealthKit access anytime via Settings > Health > LeiLeMe

## 隐私政策 — 累了么

*最后更新：2026年4月10日*

### 概述

累了么（"本应用"）是一款iOS每日恢复评估工具。本隐私政策说明本应用访问哪些数据、如何使用数据，以及你的权利。

**简短版本：** 你的所有数据都保留在你的设备上。我们不收集、传输或共享任何个人信息。

### 我们访问的数据

#### 健康数据（通过Apple HealthKit）

经你授权，本应用从Apple Health读取以下数据：

- **静息心率** — 用于评估心血管恢复状况
- **心率变异性（HRV）** — 用于评估自主神经系统恢复状况
- **睡眠分析** — 用于评估睡眠质量和时长

这些数据：
- **仅读取** — 本应用不向Apple Health写入数据
- **本地处理** — 绝不传输到设备之外
- **可选** — 本应用无需HealthKit授权即可运行（9项指标中有6项不需要）

#### 评估数据

本应用使用Apple的SwiftData框架将你的每日评估结果（反应时间、点击速度、握力、平衡、体感精力、情绪和恢复评分）存储在设备的本地数据库中。

这些数据：
- **仅本地存储** — 在你的设备存储中
- **绝不传输** — 本应用不发出网络请求
- **可导出** — 你可以随时通过设置以CSV或JSON格式导出所有数据
- **可删除** — 卸载本应用将移除所有存储的数据

### 我们不收集的数据

- 无个人身份信息（姓名、邮箱、手机号）
- 无设备标识符（IDFA、IDFV）
- 无位置数据
- 无使用分析或遥测
- 无崩溃报告发送到外部服务
- 无广告标识符
- 无Cookie或网络追踪

### 第三方共享

**无。** 我们不与第三方共享任何数据。本应用中没有第三方SDK、分析框架或广告网络。

### 追踪

本应用**不会**跨其他公司的应用或网站追踪你。本应用不使用App Tracking Transparency框架，因为没有任何内容需要追踪。

### 数据存储与安全

- 所有数据使用Apple的SwiftData框架本地存储在你的设备上
- 数据受设备内置安全功能保护（密码、Face ID、Touch ID）
- 无云同步，本应用不进行远程备份
- iCloud设备备份可能根据你的设备设置包含应用数据

### 你的权利

- **访问：** 随时通过设置 > 导出数据导出你的所有数据
- **删除：** 卸载本应用即可删除所有数据，或使用重新校准基线重置评估历史
- **可携带性：** 导出支持CSV和JSON格式
- **撤回授权：** 随时通过设置 > 健康 > 累了么撤销HealthKit访问权限
